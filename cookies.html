Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This page explains the cookies and similar technologies that may be involved when you use the CodeSimplr website.

Current Website Cookies

CodeSimplr uses Vercel Web Analytics for aggregate page and action measurement. The current integration does not enable Vercel's optional analytics cookie mode, and CodeSimplr does not use advertising or account cookies.

Browser Storage

The site uses local storage for a random website visitor identifier and session storage for a random session identifier and campaign attribution. These values support CodeSimplr's first-party funnel measurement when its signup and event database is configured. They are not used to store names, email addresses, or project messages, and they are not shared with Vercel as custom event fields.

Third-Party Services

The site loads Google Fonts and links to WhatsApp for communication. Those providers may use their own technologies when your browser connects to them or when you open their services.

Future Updates

If we add chat, advertising, account functionality, or materially different analytics technology later, this page will be updated to describe those tools and any choices available to visitors.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or block cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some third-party services may affect fonts, links, or contact flows.
