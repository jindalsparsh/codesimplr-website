# Updating the CodeSimplr Blog

Use the blog to publish practical, market-aware content that helps potential clients solve real problems. Google recommends helpful, people-first content, so write for buyers first and use SEO structure to make the page easy to understand.

## Recommended publishing rhythm

- Publish one useful article per week.
- Update older posts when the market or your services change.
- Link each article to a relevant service, country page, or the contact page.
- Keep each post specific: one audience, one problem, one useful answer.

## Good topic buckets

- AI automation examples for small businesses and agencies.
- SEO changes and practical responses for service businesses.
- Website conversion checklists and landing page teardown lessons.
- Career acceleration advice for tech, analytics, AI, and consulting roles.
- Market pages: USA, UK, Canada, UAE, and India service demand.

## How to add a post

1. Copy one file from `blog/`.
2. Rename it with a short keyword-focused slug, for example `blog/ai-agent-workflows-for-agencies.html`.
3. Update the title, meta description, canonical URL, article heading, date, and JSON-LD.
4. Add the new article to `blog.html`.
5. Add the new article URL to `sitemap.xml`.
6. Link from the article to `contact.html` and one relevant service or market page.
