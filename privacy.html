Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This policy explains how CodeSimplr handles information shared through this website, WhatsApp inquiries, and subscription requests.

Information We Collect

When you contact us, we may receive your name, email address, company, areas of interest, project details, and the message you choose to send through WhatsApp.

We also use Vercel Web Analytics to measure aggregate page visits, referral and campaign information, general device or browser categories, approximate country-level location, and selected non-sensitive actions such as CTA clicks, assessment completion, readiness score, and recommended workflow. We do not intentionally send names, email addresses, phone numbers, or free-text project messages as analytics event data.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to project inquiries and service requests.
  • To provide requested updates, proposals, or newsletter communications.
  • To improve our services, website content, and customer support.
  • To understand which pages, campaigns, and planning tools lead to relevant business inquiries.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted by Vercel and uses Vercel Web Analytics, links to WhatsApp for contact flows, and loads Google Fonts for typography. Those providers may process technical or interaction data according to their own policies when your browser connects to them or when you use their services.

Data Retention

We keep inquiry details only as long as needed to manage the relationship, provide services, or meet reasonable business and legal requirements.

Your Choices

You can ask us to update or delete information you shared with us by contacting CodeSimplr through WhatsApp.
