Common workflows

Trigger

A form submit, email, webhook, scheduled task, CRM update, or uploaded document starts the workflow.

Process

n8n cleans the data, calls AI when useful, routes the result, and writes records into the right tools.

Notify

The right person gets a clean summary, next action, or alert so work continues without manual chasing.

Why n8n fits service businesses

Service businesses usually do not need a huge software rebuild first. They need forms, messages, spreadsheets, CRMs, and AI steps to talk to each other. n8n is useful because it can connect those pieces quickly and keep the workflow visible.

The best first n8n project is usually one painful handoff: new lead intake, delayed follow-up, messy reporting, or repeated document review. CodeSimplr can audit the current process and suggest the highest-leverage workflow to automate first.
